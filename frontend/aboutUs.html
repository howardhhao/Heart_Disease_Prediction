About Us

Welcome to our heart disease risk assessment tool!

We are a team of data scientists and healthcare professionals who have developed a machine learning project aimed at providing preliminary assessments of heart disease risk.

Our project utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze key health metrics and lifestyle factors provided by users. By leveraging machine learning techniques, we can offer personalized risk assessments for individuals who may be at risk due to lifestyle habits and other factors.

Our goal is to empower users to take proactive steps towards better heart health. While our tool can provide valuable insights, it is essential to remember that it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. We encourage users to consult with their healthcare providers for comprehensive evaluations and personalized recommendations.

Thank you for using our tool to prioritize your heart health!
